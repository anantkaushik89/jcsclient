
NAME:
    delete-policy


DESCRIPTION:
    Deletes a policy for your JCS account.


SYNOPSIS:
    delete-policy --name <value> | --id <value>


OPTIONS:
    --name (string)
    The name of the policy.

    --id (string)
    The id of the policy.

EXAMPLES:
    jcs iam delete-policy --name Policy123

    jcs iam delete-policy --id 1a4e789ac85c985b3495f3495743a5e8
